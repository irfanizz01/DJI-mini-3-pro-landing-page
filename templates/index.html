About

The DJI Mini 3 Pro is a game-changer in the world of drones, combining cutting-edge technology with portability to enable creators to effortlessly capture stunning aerial footage. Emphasizing superior camera quality, extended flight time, and intelligent features, the DJI Mini 3 Pro sets a new benchmark for compact drones.

Features

  • Weight: Less than 249 g
  • Dimensions: Folded: 145×90×62 mm, Unfolded: 171×245×62 mm, Unfolded (with propellers): 251×362×70 mm
  • Max Speed: 16 m/s (S Mode), 10 m/s (N Mode), 6 m/s (C Mode)
  • Max Flight Time: 34 mins (with Intelligent Flight Battery), 47 mins (with Intelligent Flight Battery Plus)
  • Max Flight Distance: 18 km (with Intelligent Flight Battery), 25 km (with Intelligent Flight Battery Plus)
  • Camera Sensor: 1/1.3-inch CMOS, Effective Pixels: 48 MP
  • Video Resolution: 4K at 60fps, with options to use 2.7K or 1080p resolutions
  • Obstacle Sensing: Tri-Directional Obstacle Sensing
  • Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS): GPS + Galileo + BeiDou
